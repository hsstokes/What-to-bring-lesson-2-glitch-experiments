📷 Media 📊 Business ✏️ Design ⏱ ~20 min prep
This applies to everyone — Media, Business, and Design. Bring work connected to your own subject area.
1
Scanned Work from Class or Homework
Something you have already made — a sketch, layout, photograph, or page of notes. Physical work must be scanned or photographed flat. Bring at least 2–3 files.
e.g. sketchbook page · printed layout · photograph · mood board · hand-drawn type
2
A Handwritten Quote or Statement
A quote by someone you are researching — not your own words. Write it by hand on plain paper and scan it. It does not need to be neat.
e.g. artist quote · designer manifesto · photographer's statement
3
Images of the Artist or Designer You Are Researching
2–3 images of the person or work connected to your quote. Save as clearly named JPEG files.
e.g. portrait · photographs of their work · exhibition images · scanned book page

If Your Work is Physical — How to Scan

1
Scan at UAL
300 dpi minimum · 600 dpi for fine detail · save as JPEG
2
Or photograph flat
Lay flat · shoot from directly above · good daylight · no shadows · phone camera is fine
3
Save accessibly
USB · Google Drive · OneDrive — not just on your phone. You need to open files in Photoshop.
4
Name clearly
yourname-sketchbook.jpg · yourname-quote.jpg · yourname-ref1.jpg
